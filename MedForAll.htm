A patients probability of survival in an emergency mostly depends on the time under which he gets treated properly. By this website we aim to create a platform by which a person can get an ambulance to him in the shortest timeperiod. We also ensure that he gets reached to the hospital in which he gets treatment properly. Based on his medical condition at the moment and the patients pre-existing medical conditions, he is taken to a suitable hospital in which required equipment to treat him, surgeons are available. Thereby we move forward with our goal to save the lives of many.
